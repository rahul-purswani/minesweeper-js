Instructions to play: The pupose of this game is to flag all the cells containing a bomb. You can start by randomly clicking on the cells hoping you don't click on the cell containing a bomb. Every non-bomb cell you click on will tell you the total number of bombs in the eight neighboring cells. If you think that a cell contains a bomb, you can flag it by clicking on the scroll button on your mouse. If you click on the cell containing a bomb, YOU LOSE! You can reload the page to start a new game.
